Ichiraku Ramen

Ever want to eat Naruto's favorite ramen? Here's how!

Ingredients

  • string/butcher's twine
  • red miso
  • white miso
  • mugi miso
  • whole slab of prok belly (chashu)
  • ginger
  • garlic
  • scallion
  • shoyu
  • sake
  • sugar
  • water
  • shoyu
  • mirin
  • togarashi
  • sesame seeds
  • niboshi (dried small fish)
  • eggs
  • ice bath
  • pork femur bones
  • chicken carcass bones
  • chicken feet
  • prok fat back
  • cabbage
  • onion
  • carrot
  • half an apple
  • potato
  • kombu
  • dried shiitake mushroom
  • dried bonito flakes
  • narutomaki
  • ramen noodles
  • nori sheets
  • bamboo topping (menma)

Steps

Char siu (pork belly)

  1. Trim off excess fat and meat from pork belly slab.
  2. Cut off skin
  3. Roll up pork belly and tie it down with butcher's twine/string. Keep the rolled porkbelly tight to make tying easier! Keep seam side of pork belly down!
  4. Sear pork roll in a pot until edges are golden brown.
  5. Add chopped ginger, garlic, scallions.
  6. Pour in shoyu, sake, water, and sugar.
  7. Simmer pork roll for 2-3 hours.

Miso tare

  1. Combine red miso, white miso, mugi miso into small saucepan over medium heat.
  2. Add grated/minced ginger and garlic.
  3. Add shoyu, sake, mirin, togarashi, sesame seeds.
  4. Cook for a few minutes to release the rawness and thicken the consistency.

Dashi

  1. Remove heads from niboshi
  2. Pour fish into pot with water
  3. Add kombu and one shiitake mushroom.
  4. Let it sit for 10-15 minutes to rehydrate the ingredients
  5. After a little bit of gentle heat add dried bonito flakes and let it slowly simmer.
  6. Strain out all the ingredients.

Eggs

  1. Poke hole in eggs using needle. Be careful not to break the eggs!
  2. Boil in boiling water for 6.5 minutes.
  3. Remove eggs and place them in ice bath to shock the eggs.
  4. Peel eggs.

Char siu (pork belly) and eggs

  1. After three hours remove pork roll from the pot.
  2. Remove fat from the top of the marinade in the pot.
  3. Pour the liquid into a container with the eggs.
  4. Place paper towel over the container to keep eggs under the liquid.
  5. Place eggs in fridge for around a day to get nice coloring.

Broth

  1. Add pork femur bones, chicken carcass,chicken feet, water, and pork fat back to pot.
  2. Boil until meat starts to become a little tender and begins to fall off the bone.
  3. Add chopped cabbage, onion, carrot, half a whole head of garlic, scallions, ginger, half and apple, and one potato.
  4. Simmer for 2-3 hours.
  5. Add dashi to broth.
  6. Strain entire pot of broth into container.

Toppings

  1. Take of strings from pork roll and cut into rounds.
  2. Slice scallions.
  3. Slice narutomaki.
  4. Cut eggs in half.
  5. Open bamboo toppings and nori strips.

Noodles

  1. Boil noodles.
  2. Add miso tare to bowl.
  3. Add generous amount of soup to bowl and stir.
  4. Add noodles to soup.
  5. Add toppings.

Enjoy Naruto's favorite ramen!
