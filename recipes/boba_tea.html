Boba Tea

Make your own boba tea with black tea, brown sugar, and tapioca balls!

Ingredients

  • Black Tea
  • Brown sugar
  • Water
  • Tapica balls
  • Ice

Steps

  1. Steep tea in hot water.
  2. Dissolve brown sugar in hot water to make brown sugar syrup.
  3. Make tapioca balls by boiling in water.
  4. Spoon tapioca balls and brown sugar syrup into cup.
  5. Add tea and ice then stir.

Enjoy your drink
