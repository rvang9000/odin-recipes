Drunken Noodles

Drunken noodles (pad kee mao) is a thai dish made with rice noodles, protein, and veggies. This is an easy dish made in 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • Wok
  • Something to stir with

Protein

  • 8 oz choice of protein
  • 1 tsp fish sauce
  • 1 clove garlic grated
  • White/black pepper

Sauce

  • 2 tbsp oyster sauce
  • 1 tbsp fish sauce
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp palm sugar
  • White/black pepper

Stir fry

  • 2 tbsp cooking oil
  • 8 cloves garlic chopped
  • 10 thai chili (or as much as you want)chopped
  • 1 large shallot sliced
  • 6 oz chinese broccoli (or someother leafy green veggie) chopped into bite size pieces
  • 1 lb noodles
  • 1 red chili/red bellpepper
  • handful of basil

Steps

Protein

Begin by marinating the protein

  1. Place protein into a bowl and add 1 tsp fish sauce.
  2. Add white/black pepper
  3. Add 1 grated clove of garlic

Set the protein aside while we prepare everything else.

Sauce

  1. Begin with 2 tbsp of oyster sauce
  2. Next add 1 tbsp of fish sauce
  3. Add 1 tbsp soy sauce
  4. Add 2 tbsp of palm sugar to the sauce
  5. Add a pinch of white/black pepper
  6. Finally mix it all together

Stir Fry

  1. Begin by boiling the noodles
  2. Start chopping 8 cloves of garlic
  3. Chop the 10 thai chilis
  4. Slice 1 shallot and set it aside with garlic and chili
  5. Chop up the red chili/bellpepper
  6. Chop up the 6 oz of chinese broccoli
  7. pull of basil leaves

Cooking

  1. Heat up wok until it starts to smoke then add 2 tbsp of oil
  2. Add shallot, garlic, and chili
  3. Then add the protein until it is nice and golden brown
  4. Add chinese broccoli and cook for 1-2 minutes or until your preferred doneness
  5. Add noodles and sauce
  6. Stir until noodles are cooked in sauce
  7. Add thai chili and red basil

And you're done! Enjoy your drunken noodles.
